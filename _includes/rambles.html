rambles

I rememeber being asked by my supervisor at work to walk him through a piece of code I had just written. The task seemed easy enough, afterall, I wrote the code myself. Funny enough, I found I was just rambling and spitting back each line of code to him most of the time. It wasn't that I didn't understand what I had just written, I just lacked the right vocabulary to explain my code in the simplest of terms.

From this realization, I decided to task myself with writing about some of my experiences and interests, in the simplest of terms. Some of these writings might seem like tutorials (I can't help it, I need to include some code), however, they are just intended as a way to practice putting some of my thoughts and actions into writing (well... fine... it's a blog).
